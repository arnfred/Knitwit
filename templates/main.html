Knitwit

Create your own knitting pattern

I've made this page to help people create knitting patterns from photos or images. The process is simple; you upload an image, adjust it, adjust the gauge and generate a pattern. Once you've made the pattern you can save it and share it so others can use and improve it.

I've included a short guide on how to use Knitwit on my blog. You can see the pattern example from the blog post here
